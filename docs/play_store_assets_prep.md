# Google Play Store Assets Preparation

## Metadata Details

### Short Description
Secure, offline-first personal ledger and expense bookkeeping manager.

### Full Description
KhataFlow is a modern, lightweight, and secure personal ledger application designed to help freelancers, small businesses, and individuals manage financial relationships without an internet connection.

Key Features:
- **100% Offline-First**: All ledger balances, transaction journals, and customer lists are saved locally on your device.
- **Biometric Security**: Protect your data with hardware-backed biometric authentication (Fingerprint/Face Unlock) and secure 4-digit PIN setup.
- **Professional PDF Statements**: Compile customer transaction histories into cleanly structured PDF statements, ready to share via WhatsApp, email, or print.
- **Due Date Reminders**: Keep track of payables and receivables with automated notifications and reminders.
- **Interactive Analytics**: Monitor cash flow margins and net position balances on a modern, customizable dashboard.
- **Trash Bin Recovery**: Safe deletion engine with a 30-day auto-purge frame to restore deleted items.
